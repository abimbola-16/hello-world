About

GIRLS'S BRIGADE

GB LAGOS is part of a nationwide Christian movement that has partnered with churches for years.
Together we are committed to seeing girls’ lives transformed and God’s world enriched.
Together we help girls and leaders seek, serve and follow Christ.

History

The founder of Girls Brigade was Late Mrs Margaret Beauty.
Girls Brigade came into the Northern part of Nigeria in the year 1942
in 1949 it came into the Western Nigeria while it came into the Eastern part of Nigeria in 1951.
